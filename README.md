# 🍑 aSlap: The App That Wants to Be Smacked

Welcome to **aSlap**, the only Android application that turns your expensive smartphone into a high-tech feedback loop for your most questionable impulses. Whether you're feeling lonely, frustrated, or just have a hand that's itching for action, aSlap is here to... *respond*.

## 🫦 What is this?
aSlap uses your device's high-precision accelerometer to detect sudden, forceful movements. When it detects a "slap" (or a vigorous shake, we don't judge), it rewards you with:
*   **Audio Satisfaction:** A random selection from our library of 60 high-quality... *vocalizations* and sound effects.
*   **Physical Feedback:** Heavy haptic vibrations that make you feel like the phone is actually feeling it.
*   **Visual Ripples:** A juicy peach UI that pulses with every impact.

## ✨ Features
*   **Foreground Monitoring:** Our "Armed & Ready" service stays active in the background. Keep your phone in your pocket and wait for the right moment.
*   **The Peach (🍑):** A central interactive target for those times when you can't be physical and just want to tap that.
*   **Cooldown Logic:** We aren't masochists. There's a 500ms cooldown between slaps to ensure every strike is properly appreciated.
*   **One-Touch Termination:** Feeling guilty? Use the "Stop" button in the notification or the UI to put the app to sleep.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
*   **Jetpack Compose:** For that smooth, rippling peach experience.
*   **Foreground Services:** To ensure the fun doesn't stop just because you switched apps.
*   **Sensor APIs:** Tuning the accelerometer to exactly 2.5G of "love."
*   **MediaPlayer & Vibrator:** For the sensory overload you didn't know you needed.
